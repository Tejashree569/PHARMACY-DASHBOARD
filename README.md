## 📌 Project Overview

The Pharmacy Inventory Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to monitor inventory performance, revenue, profit, product categories, and order status. The dashboard provides valuable business insights through dynamic charts, KPI cards, and interactive filters, enabling data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance
- Analyze monthly revenue and profit trends
- Track inventory quantity and product categories
- Evaluate order status performance
- Identify revenue contribution by product category
- Improve inventory management using data visualization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- ⭐ Total Rating
- 📦 Total Quantity
- 💰 Total Revenue
- 📈 Total Profit

### Visualizations
- Monthly Revenue by Product Category
- Monthly Profit Growth (Waterfall Chart)
- Revenue by Order Status
- Order Status Distribution
- Monthly Profit Trend
- Top Product Categories Over Time

### Interactive Filters
- City Filter
- Dynamic Cross Filtering
- Drill-down Visualizations

## 📈 Key Insights

- Monitored monthly revenue and profit performance.
- Compared revenue across different product categories.
- Tracked inventory quantity and business growth.
